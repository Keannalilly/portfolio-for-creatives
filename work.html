  • About Me

    In Jan 2019, I was diagnosed with Myelodysplastic Syndrome, a rare blood cancer. With a supportive friend by my side, I faced treatments and uncertainties. Amidst struggles and loss, I found solace in planning for a life-saving stem cell transplant. Inspired by my journey, I turned to Twitch streaming and pursued a degree in Multimedia Communications. Now, armed with newfound skills and purpose, I'm ready to inspire and educate others through my story.
